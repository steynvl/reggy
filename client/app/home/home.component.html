What is reggy?

Reggy is a regular expression generator that assists you with the construction of regular expressions. The generator consists of three components, each for a different use case. Reggy generates regular expressions for a wide variety of programming languages and strives to generate regular expressions that are as efficient and concise as possible.

Sample-based Generation

Generates regular expressions by letting you provide one or more sample strings. You then highlight different parts of the sample strings and provides the system with extra information about every highlighted pair, such as the type of the text, repetition information, and lookaround information.

Common Use Cases

Provides you with a generalised regular expression for common use cases, specifically, cases that frequently arise in practice. This includes email addresses, date and time, passwords, currencies, credit card numbers, etc.

Regular Expression Learning

This component extends Reggy to the induction of regular langages. We expose the automata learning library Inferrer, which provides algorithms for learning regular languages from example strings. We also provide an online interactive learning environment, by letting you act as a minimally adequate Teacher in the L* learner-teacher setup.
